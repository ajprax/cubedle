How it works:

  1. Cards are rated using the Glicko-2 system based on head-to-head votes
  2. Higher-rated cards are more likely to be included in the final cube
  3. New cards start with neutral ratings and move up or down based on votes
  4. Cards with higher uncertainty are more likely to appear in voting pairs
